Habiting — Delete Account & Data

How to delete your account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app:

Step 1: Open the Habiting app
Step 2: Go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation)
Step 3: Tap "Izrakstīties" (Sign out) to sign out of your account
Step 4: Your cloud data in Firebase will no longer be linked to an active session
⚠️ Note: Signing out removes access to cloud-synced data. Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

How to delete your data

To delete all local data stored on your device:

Option 1: Go to your device Settings → Apps → Habiting → Clear Data
Option 2: Uninstall the Habiting app — all local data will be permanently removed

What data is deleted

  • Account information — email address, display name
  • Habits and categories — all custom habits, categories, icons, and colors
  • Tracking history — all habit entries and completion records
  • Reminders — all scheduled notifications
  • Settings — theme preferences, sync settings
  • Cloud data (if Premium) — synced data in Firebase Firestore
